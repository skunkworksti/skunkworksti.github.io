Project Theseus

The story changes
before the investigation starts.

By the time a formal QA investigation convenes, the timeline has already been revised. Details soften. What actually happened gets replaced by what people think happened — or what they're comfortable reporting.

Theseus deploys before that collapse. Before the investigation team assembles. While the details are still fresh, uncollapsed, and contradictory in the right ways.

The Method

Team Learning Session.
Six dimensions. One record.

A structured AI-assisted session that captures the incident through the 6W framework — before positions harden and narratives converge.

W1
What happened
W2
Who was involved
W3
When it occurred
W4
Where it happened
W5
Why it occurred
H
How it unfolded
The Deliverable

The investigation team
inherits a record, not a story.

Theseus produces a structured four-layer Investigator's Package — handed directly to the QA team when they convene.

01
Executive Dashboard
High-level incident summary for leadership and review boards.
02
Interactive Timeline
Reconstructed sequence of events with source attribution.
03
Divergence Report
Where accounts conflict. Where the gaps are. What to investigate first.
04
Full Transcripts
Complete session record — auditable, attributable, preserved.
