Project Recall

Institutional Memory, Preserved

Experienced operators carry decades of undocumented knowledge — failure modes, workarounds, judgment calls that can't be found in any manual. Project Recall captures it before it's gone.

Methodology

The Capture Process

AI-assisted interviews follow the operator's expertise — not a generic checklist. Each session produces a structured knowledge artifact: indexed, searchable, and ready for the next person who needs it.

Operational Results

What Gets Preserved

Fault diagnosis logic. Maintenance judgment. Historical context that explains why the current procedure exists. The institutional knowledge that makes the difference between a trained worker and an expert one.
